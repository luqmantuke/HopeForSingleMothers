About and Causes of Hope For Single Mothers

Hope For Single Mothers was founded in 2018, our main goal was and still lighting the path for single mothers in Tanzania. They are facing a lot and by themselves can't handle all these together. More especially raising and taking care of the family, meanwhile they have no money or skills to help them out in their daily basis.

Our overall goal is to help single mothers to know their rights , empower and build women to be very creative in enterpreneurship, leadership and gender equality. Our core values are: DIGNITY, UNITY IN DIVERSITY, TEAMWORK, CARING, ACCOUNTABILITY AND INTEGRITY.
